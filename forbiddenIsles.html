The Forbidden Isles

A curious isle that turns away all who attempt to enter its domain

Overview

It's name a consequence of the curious phenomenon surrounding the collection of islands, the Forbidden isles lay to the west of the continent. Most sailors attempting to blaze a trail to these islands find themselves inexplicably diverted, their ships caught in enigmatic currents and uncanny winds that mysteriously guide them back to the mainland. Navigational instruments fail, compasses spin wildly, and the stars seem to shift their positions in the sky, leaving seasoned navigators bewildered.
