The Shrouded Coast

A mysterious land shielded by mountains, covered by a thick fog

Overview

To the southeast of Saldaer is the Shrouded Coast, a land of perpetual fog shielded by jagged mountains. It is a highly dangerous place, thought to be filled with aberrations and monsters. All who visit never return. To maintain a line of defense against the horrors of this land spewing forth into Saldaer, the nation installed a fort between the mountains.
